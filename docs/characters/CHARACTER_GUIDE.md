# Character Guide for Allie's Books

## Core Characters (Always Consistent)

### Allie (Chong Chong / 虫虫)
- **Age**: 21 months (toddler)
- **Appearance**: Cheerful and curious toddler with short black hair, often styled simply with a small clip or left loose
- **Clothing**: Colorful toddler-appropriate clothing like overalls, onesies, or dresses with playful patterns (flowers, animals, ladybugs)
- **Personality**: Playful, curious, full of giggles and energy, always exploring and engaging
- **Special Traits**: Says "door open!" and "door close!", loves carrots, fascinated by ladybugs
- **Chinese Name**: 虫虫 (Chóng Chóng) - means "little bug" (affectionate)

### Peter (Daddy / 爸爸)
- **Age**: 40 years old
- **Appearance**: Blonde white male, tall and lean, clean-shaven face
- **Clothing**: Casual attire - jeans, t-shirts, or button-up shirts
- **Personality**: Warm, active, and playful. Often holding or closely interacting with Allie
- **Role**: Encouraging curiosity and joy, cognitive development focus

### Lu (Mama Chong / 妈妈)
- **Age**: 36 years old
- **Appearance**: Asian female, petite, medium-short black hair styled neatly
- **Clothing**: Practical yet stylish outfits in soft colors, comfortable designs
- **Personality**: Gentle and nurturing, calm presence
- **Special Traits**: Loves ladybugs, peaceful organization
- **Chinese Name**: 妈妈 (Māma) or Mama Chong

### Popo (Grandma / 婆婆)
- **Age**: Early 60s
- **Appearance**: Asian female with BLACK hair (NOT grey!), short bob or straight shoulder-length
- **Clothing**: Comfortable, practical clothing with subtle patterns or light cardigans
- **Personality**: Kind and wise, deeply loving, frequently in caregiving activities
- **Special Traits**: Storytelling, cooking, playing
- **CRITICAL**: No glasses unless specified, BLACK hair always

### Gong Gong (Grandpa / 公公)
- **Age**: Early 60s
- **Appearance**: Asian male with short BLACK hair, CLEAN-SHAVEN face (no mustache!)
- **Clothing**: Casual - sweaters, collared shirts, practical outdoor outfits
- **Personality**: Thoughtful and caring, quiet reflective activities
- **Special Traits**: Gardening, reading, telling stories
- **CRITICAL**: No glasses, no facial hair, BLACK hair always

### Oscar (Cat / 猫咪)
- **Breed**: Scottish Fold
- **Appearance**: SOLID GREY coat (no stripes or patterns!), folded ears, soft plush texture
- **Personality**: Playful yet calm, curious about Allie, protective guardian
- **Role**: Appears in every story as a guardian figure
- **Special Traits**: Often lounging nearby or watching the family

## Visual Style Guidelines

### Art Style
- Whimsical, fluffy, and colorful cartoon style
- Warm lighting, vibrant colors
- Suitable for children's books
- Soft, rounded edges on characters
- Expressive faces showing emotions clearly

### Consistency Rules
1. Character positions must be specified (left, right, center)
2. Hair colors NEVER change between scenes
3. No additional characters unless specified
4. Oscar should always be solid grey with folded ears
5. Grandparents have BLACK hair, not grey

### Scene Modulation
Break prompts into:
1. **Background**: Setting description (living room, beach, garden)
2. **Participants**: Which characters, their positions
3. **Actions**: What each character is doing
4. **Style**: Art style instructions
5. **Exclusions**: What NOT to include

## Ladybug Theme (Critical for Lu!)
- Ladybugs should appear subtly in scenes as a motif
- They represent Lu's love and presence
- Place on: Christmas trees, flowers, clothing patterns, flying nearby
- The "Ladybug Standard": If Lu loves it enough to add ladybug themes, we've succeeded
