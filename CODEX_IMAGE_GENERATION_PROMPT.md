## Allie's Bilingual Children's Books - Complete Illustration Guide

**Project:** Nova's Gift to Allie
**Purpose:** Generate 100 illustrations for 5 bilingual children's books
**Target Audience:** 21-month-old toddler (Allie)

## CRITICAL CHARACTER DESCRIPTIONS

### ALLIE (Main Character)
- **Age:** 21 months old (toddler)
- **Ethnicity:** Asian-American (mixed - Asian mother, Caucasian father)
- **Hair:** Short black hair in a bob cut
- **Eyes:** Big, sparkling brown eyes
- **Face:** Round face with rosy cheeks, button nose
- **Expression:** Usually cheerful, curious, joyful
- **Clothing varies by book:**
  - Book 1: Colorful overalls, pink dress, pajamas
  - Book 2: Yellow sundress, overalls
  - Book 3: Pink dress, colorful clothes
  - Book 4: Polka-dot pajamas (key outfit!)
  - Book 5: Rainbow dress, various colored outfits matching the color being taught

### OSCAR (The Cat)
- **Breed:** Scottish Fold
- **Color:** SOLID GREY - NO stripes, NO patterns, just solid grey fur
- **Ears:** Distinctive FOLDED ears pointing forward (this is critical!)
- **Eyes:** Yellow-green eyes
- **Body:** Fluffy, plump, adorable
- **Personality:** Curious, follows Allie everywhere, occasionally surprised

### PETER (Daddy)
- **Ethnicity:** Caucasian
- **Age:** Early 40s
- **Build:** Tall, lean
- **Hair:** Blonde
- **Eyes:** Kind blue eyes
- **Face:** CLEAN-SHAVEN (no beard, no mustache)
- **Expression:** Warm smile, gentle
- **Clothing:** Casual - blue shirts, comfortable dad clothes

### LU (Mommy)
- **Ethnicity:** Chinese
- **Build:** Petite
- **Hair:** Medium-length black hair (shoulder-length)
- **Eyes:** Warm brown eyes
- **Expression:** Gentle smile, nurturing
- **Clothing:** Cozy sweaters, comfortable mom clothes

### GONG GONG (Grandfather - Lu's father)
- **Ethnicity:** Chinese
- **Age:** Early 60s
- **Hair:** Short BLACK hair (NOT grey!)
- **Face:** CLEAN-SHAVEN (NO mustache, NO beard)
- **Eyes:** Kind, NO glasses
- **Expression:** Warm, weathered face with kind wrinkles
- **Clothing:** Casual or traditional Chinese-style shirts

### POPO (Grandmother - Lu's mother)
- **Ethnicity:** Chinese
- **Age:** Early 60s
- **Hair:** Short BLACK bob (NOT grey!)
- **Face:** Warm smile with laugh lines
- **Eyes:** Gentle, NO glasses
- **Clothing:** Red traditional Chinese top, floral blouses

---

## STYLE GUIDELINES

### Overall Aesthetic
- **Style:** Warm, whimsical watercolor illustration
- **Mood:** Cozy, inviting, magical
- **Reference:** Similar to classic children's books like "Goodnight Moon" or "The Snowy Day"
- **Colors:** Soft pastel palette with gentle gradients
- **Characters:** Large expressive eyes, friendly round faces
- **Lighting:** Soft, warm lighting throughout

### Absolute Requirements
1. **NO TEXT** - No letters, words, or numbers in any illustration
2. **NO SCARY ELEMENTS** - Everything must be safe and friendly
3. **CHILD-SAFE** - Appropriate for toddlers
4. **CONSISTENT CHARACTERS** - Characters must look the same across all pages

### The Ladybug Motif
- Include a small ladybug somewhere in most illustrations
- Ladybugs are red with black spots
- They appear on windowsills, leaves, flowers, photo frames
- This is a recurring "find the ladybug" game element

### Image Format
- **Size:** 1536x1024 pixels (landscape orientation for book pages)
- **Format:** PNG
- **Quality:** High quality, suitable for printing
